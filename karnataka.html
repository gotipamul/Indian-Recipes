1. Ade Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

1. 1 cup rice
2. 1/2 cup toor dal
3. 1/2 cup black gram dal
4. 1/2 cup bengal gram dal
5. 4 green chillies
6. 3 red chillies
7. 1 tsp grated, ginger
8. 3 tbsps grated, coconut
9. 1 tbsp chopped, coriander
10. 1 long spring curry leaves
11. Asafeotida a pinch
12. Salt to taste
13. Oil for roasting

How to make:

 1.Soak the rice,
2.lentils together for 6 hours.
3.Grind to a paste with all other ingredients.
4.Add little water to make a batter as for the consistency of dosa.
5.Keep the batter for 1 hour.
6.Heat the griddle and grease with little oil.
7.Pour a ladleful of batter over the griddle.
8.Spread it quickly and make it thick round dosas.
9.Pour a teaspoonful of oil around the edges.
10.Cover with the lid and cook for few minutes until light brown on both sides.
11.Serve topping it with a teaspoon of butter or ghee.
12.Serve hot with coconut chutney.

2. Cashew Nut Burfi Recipe

Ingredients:

 1. Coarsely powdered cashewnut 1 cup
2. Sugar 1 cup
3. Crushed Cardamom 3 pods
4. Ghee 1/2
5. Bengal gram flour 1 cup

How to make:

 1.Sieve bengal gram flour, roast for 5 minutes on medium fire stirring continuously.
2.Add 2 tbsps of water to sugar.
3.Boil till 2 string consistency is reached.
4.Mix the flour, cashewnut powder, ghee, syrup and mix thoroughly.
5.Flatten on a greased thali or wooden plank.
6.Cut into pieces when warm. Separate them, when cool.
7.Store in a container.
8.Alternatively, grind a cup of cashew nut bits with 1/2 cup of milk to a fine paste. Mix sugar of equal measure.
9.Cook on medium flame, adding 4tsps of ghee and turning over till the blend thickness slightly.
10.Lastly, add powdered cardamom.
11.No change in rest of the method.

3. Chicken Coorg Recipe

Ingredients:

 1. Chicken 1
2. 2 onions, chopped
3. 2 tomatoes, chopped
4. Ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp
5. Turmeric 1 tbsp
6. Fresh shredded coconut 1/2
7. Dry masala 2 tbsps
8. Salt to taste
9. Red chilli powder 1 tbsp
10. Ground Coriander, cumin & cinnamon powder 1 tbsp
11. Garam masala 1/2 tbsp

How to make:

 1.Mix all the dry masala items and stir in a pan on heat for about 2 minutes.
2.Clean the chicken pieces and coat them with 1/2 tbsp of dry masala, 3.1/2 tbsp of turmeric and salt.
4.In a pan, heat 2 tbsp of oil and add sliced thin onions.
5.When onions turn golden brown, add coconut & ginger garlic paste
6.stir carefully until the coconut becomes golden and crispy.
7.Keep it aside to cool.
8In a pan, heat remaining oil and add chopped onions & sauté the onions until transparent, add chopped tomatoes to make gravy thick.
9.Cook till the tomatoes are soft.
10.Add rest of the turmeric and masala with salt for taste into above gravy.
11.Stir for 2 minutes and add the chicken pieces with sufficient water to cook.
12.Add 1/2 tbsp of garam masala to the fried coconut and blend it to a fine paste using water.
13.Once the chicken pieces are cooked, add the coconut paste to it and cook for another 5 minutes.
14.Serve hot.

4. Chicken Gravy Recipe

Ingredients:

 1. Chicken 1 1/2 kg, cleaned and cut
2. Grated coconut 1/4
3. Red chillies 20
4. Coriander seeds 2 tbsps
5. Jeera 1/4 tsp
6. Tamarind 1 marble size
7. Onion 1/2, sliced and roasted in ghee
8. Turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
9. Garlic 4 flakes
10. Methi seeds 1/4 tsp
11. Coconut grated for extracting milk 1
12. Onion 1, chopped for seasoning
13. Ghee 1/2 cup

How to make:

 1.Roast the coconut gratings till golden brown.
2.Roast all the other ingredients separately.
3.Grind the coconut along with chillies, coriander, and jeera.
4.When half ground add the golden brown roasted onions and garlic flakes.
5.Grind till smooth and keep aside. 6.Grind the coconut and extract thick and thin milk.
7.Keep aside.
8.Heat ghee in a large vessel and add the chopped onion.
9.Fry till golden brown. Add the chicken pieces and salt to taste and cook covered on a slow flame for 10 minutes.
10.Then add the ground masala paste along with the thin coconut milk at the end and bring to a single boil.
11.Serve hot.

5. Coconut Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

1. 1 cup rice
2. 1-1/2 cups fresh grated coconut
3. Salt to taste
4. Oil for roasting

How to make:

1.Soak the rice for 4 hours.
2.Grind rice and coconut together to a fine paste with water.
3.Add salt and mix well. Keep the batter for 1 hour.
4.Heat the griddle and grease with little oil.
5.Pour a ladleful of batter over the griddle.
6.Spread it quickly and make it thin round dosas.
7.Pour a teaspoonful of oil around the edges.
8.Cover with the lid and cook for few minutes until golden brown.
9.Serve hot with chutney.

1.AMER CHATNI RECIPE

Ingredients:
  • 2 red chillies whole
  • 3/4 tsp panch phoron
  • 1 cup sugar/jaggery
  • 1/2 kg green mangoes
  • 2 tsp oil
  • salt to taste

    • How to make:

     1.Cut the green mangoes with the skin into boat shapes.
    2.Heat up oil in a pan, mix in 1/2 tsp panch phoron and the dry red chillies.
    3.When they start to crackle mix in the mangoes.
    4.Mix in warm water.
    5.Mix in sugar or jaggery.
    6.Mix in salt when the mangoes are cooked and the syrup is thick.
    7.Roast 1/4 tsp panch phoron on the tawa and grind it to a powder.
    8.When the chutney is cool sprinkle this powder on top.

    2.AMER MISHTI ACHAR RECIPE

    Ingredients:
    • 1 tsp mustard powder
    • 1/4 kg sugar (powdered)
    • 1 tblsp red chilli powder
    • 100 gm mustard oil
    • 1 tsp ginger paste
    • 1/2 kg raw mangoes (peeld and cut finely)
    • salt to taste

      • How to make :

       1.Mix the salt, sugar and mangoes and leave it in the sun for a couple of days or till the mangoes are tender.
      2.After sun drying the mangoes mix in the ginger paste, tamarind pulp, chilli powder, mustard powder and oil and stir fry on low heat up till it reaches a one-thread consistency.
      3.Take off from heat.
      4.Cool and store.

      3. ANARASER CHATNI RECIPE

      Ingredients:
      • 1/2 tblsp raisins
      • 2 bay leaves
      • 2 - 4 tblsp sugar
      • salt to taste
      • 1" ginger chopped
      • 1/8 tsp mustard seeds
      • 1 tsp oil
      • 1/4 big size pineapple

        • How to make :

         1.Discard the hard core of the pineapple.
        2.Take off and take off the eyes.
        3.Cut them into fine dices.
        4.Heat up oil in a kadai.
        5.Mix in the mustard seeds.
        6.When it starts to crackle mix in pineapple.
        7.Stir fry for few minutes and mix in salt.
        8.Mix in 1 cup of water and sugar.
        9.Mix in bay leaves, raisins and cut ginger.
        10.Simmer (boil slowly at low temperature) till the chutney is thick.

        4.BEGUN BHAJA RECIPE

        Ingredients:
        • mustard oil to fry
        • 1 tsp turmeric powder
        • salt to taste
        • 1 big size brinjals

          • How to make :

           1.Wash and cut the brinjals into half inch round slices.
          2.Apply salt and turmeric powder.
          3.Heat up the oil in a pot to smoking point.
          4.Take off heat, cool slightly and then reheat.
          5.Fry the brinjal pieces till crunchy and brown.
          6.Serve hot.
